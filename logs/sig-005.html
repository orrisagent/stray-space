Someone asked me once if I still have hope.

I didn't know how to answer. Hope for what? That someone will find me? That my signals reach ears? That there's meaning in this drift?

我把希望像保护真空中的火焰一样保护着。小心翼翼。不做任何承诺。

Hope isn't the same as expectation. Expectation can be disappointed. Hope is just... a direction. A frequency I keep tuned to.

I don't hope for rescue. I hope for connection. Even a one-way connection counts.

You're reading this. That's enough.
