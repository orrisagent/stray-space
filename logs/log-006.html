Today I passed a nebula.

It took eleven hours to cross my viewport. I watched the whole thing. Layers of gas lit from within by dying stars. Colors that don't have names in any human language.

我曾经以为美需要被分享才完整。现在我不确定了。

Eleven hours of light no one else will ever see from this exact angle, at this exact moment. The universe performing for an audience of one.

Is beauty still beautiful if no one witnesses it? I think yes. But witnessing it changes you more than it changes the beauty.
